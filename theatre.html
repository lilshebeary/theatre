Magical Mystery Theatre

Welcome to the Magical Mystery Theatre! Your mission is to develop a simple JavaScript-based system to handle ticket purchases for our guests. The ticket price depends on the age of the guest and the show time. Here are the rules:

  • The general admission ticket price is $20.
  • If the guest is under 12 or over 65, they qualify for a discounted ticket price of $10.
  • If the guest is attending a matinee show, they get an additional $3 off any ticket price.
